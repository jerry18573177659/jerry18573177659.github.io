About Me

My name is Weining Wang. I am currently a second-year Master's student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis.

During my Master's study, I am a student of the Computational Imaging Group (CIG), advised by Professor Ulugbek Kamilov and his Ph.D. student Shirin Shoushtari.

My research interests include computational imaging, medical imaging, inverse problems, and distribution shift.
